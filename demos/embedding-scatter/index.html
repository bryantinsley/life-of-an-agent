Honest caveat: the famous king − man + woman ≈ queen result was much cleaner in older word-embedding models (word2vec, GloVe) trained directly for that property. In modern LLM input-embedding tables it's fuzzier — it works for some pairs and not others. The point isn't that the geometry is perfectly clean; it's that the direction "male → female" is detectable as a vector at all.
