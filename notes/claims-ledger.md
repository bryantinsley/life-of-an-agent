**Sweep date:** 2026-04-20
**Scope:** Every Gemini / Google-infra claim currently in `course_master_outline.md`, plus the TPU-vs-GPU architectural block slated for consolidation into S5.
**Purpose:** A dated, sourced snapshot used (a) to recalibrate hedging language in all 10 sessions before production and (b) as the baseline cross-reference for per-session §10.2 week-of revalidations.

## Methodology

1. Pull each Gemini / Google-infra claim verbatim (or lightly paraphrased) from the outline, grouped by session.
2. Primary sources first: Google DeepMind / Google AI blog posts, model cards, technical-report PDFs, Vertex AI / AI-for-Developers docs. Academic papers (arXiv) for non-Google foundational claims (Mixtral, lost-in-the-middle, PagedAttention, GPT-3).
3. Reputable secondary where primary is thin: SemiAnalysis, Epoch AI, mainstream tech press (CNBC, The Register). Avoided LLM-generated summaries as sources.
4. Each claim assigned a status — `confirmed` / `widely-believed` / `speculation` / `outdated` — plus a pull quote (verbatim where a single sentence nails it; "synthesis" where a conclusion rests on multiple sources).
5. Corrected hedging language is a one-sentence rewrite that is accurate at the 2026-04-20 snapshot and written to age gracefully (names the era of claim, not the exact version when that risks drift).

Treat as "publicly thin" in the deck; do not assert specific implementation details.

## Naming-and-versioning notes (things that have shifted since the outline was drafted)

- **"Flash, Pro, Ultra" is stale.** The current Gemini tier ladder (as of April 2026) is **Pro → Flash → Flash-Lite**. "Ultra" was the top tier in Gemini 1.0 (late 2023 / early 2024) and has not been used as a shipping tier name since. In 1.5, 2.0, 2.5, and 3.x, only Pro and Flash (and later Flash-Lite) appear. Rewrite the S3 callout to use "Flash-Lite, Flash, Pro."
- **Gemini is on version 3.x.** The outline's reading-list line "Google's Gemini technical reports (1, 1.5, 2+)" should be expanded to include Gemini 2.5 (tech report published 2025) and Gemini 3 Pro / Flash model cards (November–December 2025). Gemini 3.1 Pro shipped February 2026; Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite shipped March 2026.
- **Agentspace → Gemini Enterprise.** On October 9, 2025, Google folded Agentspace into Gemini Enterprise. Agentspace is being discontinued for sale on December 31, 2025 (existing customers grandfathered under their contracts). The S9 Google/Gemini callout should say "Gemini Enterprise (formerly Agentspace) + Vertex AI Gen AI Evaluation Service."
- **MoE is confirmed, not speculated.** The S5 framing "widely speculated to be MoE, not confirmed" is two years out of date — Gemini 1.5 (2024), 2.5 (2025), and 3 Pro (2025) all explicitly describe themselves as sparse MoE in their tech reports / model cards. What remains undisclosed is expert count, router details, and active-vs-total parameter ratios — hedge on *those*, not on MoE itself.
- **TPU generations referenced for Gemini training:** confirmed per primary source — v4 for Gemini 1.5, v5p for Gemini 2.5. Gemini 3 training generation is reported in secondary coverage as v5e + v6e (Trillium / Ironwood family); flag for §10.2 recheck against primary sources.
- **"Exaflop-days" is colloquial.** Primary sources (Epoch AI, tech reports) report training compute in raw FLOPs; GPT-3 used "petaflop/s-days"; "exaflop-days" does appear in industry discussion but is not the convention in the papers themselves. Either use FLOPs directly (10²⁵–10²⁶) or present "exaflop-days" as an informal shorthand and define the conversion.
